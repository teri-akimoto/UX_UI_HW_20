About

UX/UI Designer & Illustrator based in Fountain Valley, California

Lan has a keen interest in the UI branding and language used to communicate with the users, and attention to detail in micro-interactions. The user’s experience can be truly delightful when these elements are considered, as demonstrated in the Endangered Species Redesign.

She also dabbles in watercolour in her free time as "Teri Akimoto" and loves to apply both the medium’s subtlety and vibrancy in her designs. A selection can be found in Illustrations.

UX Skills

User Research

Framing a specific problem helps her jumpstart into testing.

Thorough research into the user’s experience with competitors’ solutions has helped her team to identify their weaknesses that they can explore and build upon.

User Empathy

Current pain points and user demographics help inform on who the users are and what they need.

User personas, problem statements and storyboards are her go-to tools for user empathy.

Qualitative Interviews

Interviews have shaped the presented case studies by bringing out interesting insights, e.g. the users’ frustrations to navigate through a visually pleasing website.
